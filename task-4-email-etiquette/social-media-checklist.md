Social Media Do's and Don'ts
For College Students
✅ Do's
Keep your profiles clean and professional
Think before you post — ask yourself if your professor or future employer would approve
Use social media to showcase your skills and achievements
Follow industry experts and professionals in your field
Keep your privacy settings updated and reviewed regularly
❌ Don'ts
Never share your personal details like phone number or address publicly
Never post offensive, racist, or inappropriate content
Never share confidential information about your college or classmates
Never engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
